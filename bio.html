Music

I spent much of my youth writing and performing rap. I never had any real desire to succeed in that business, but used it more as a form of light therapy. My first song was recorded in my childhood bedroom in early 2007 at the age of 15. It was something kept mostly to myself until 2009 when I started meeting like-minded people popping up around me. This hobby is now mostly defunct, the video above being from 2019, also being the latest/last thing I put out.
